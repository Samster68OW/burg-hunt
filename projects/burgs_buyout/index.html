How to Play

Burg needs our help! We need to raise coins to repair the Migrator after it hit an iceberg. Click the large coin on the left to collect coins. Then reinvest those coins into hiring penguins to play minigames for you. Good luck!

Controls

Press the Spacebar to earn coins.
Press Q to toggle the middle column.
Press W to toggle the right column.
Hold Shift to purchase as many minigames as you can in one click.

Welcome to the...


All of your minigames, upgrades, and puffles have been lost to the void.

Your coins that entered this place have turned into Doubloons, which can be spent in the box dimension on Ascended Upgrades. These upgrades are more powerful and persist across ascensions. Click a node on the right to purchase the ascended upgrade.

Your coins also increase your Box Level, which also persists across ascensions. Each Box Level grants a +10% boost to your CPS and to your coins earned per click.
