Burg's Scavenger Hunt


Welcome!

Welcome to Burg's Scavenger Hunt! This is a fun activity to play while waddling around Club Penguin Zero. Burg lost some of Captain Rockhopper's cargo and needs your help to locate it! By using the clues provided, you will need to explore Club Penguin Island and identify the locations of his cargo. You cannot fail a hunt, so relax, take your time, and have fun! Please keep in mind that this project is not affiliated with Club Penguin Zero and is made by fans.

About


Burg's Scavenger Hunt was conceptualized in January of 2026 on the Zero Nation Discord server. When scavenger hunts are available, players may start one on this website while exploring Club Penguin Island. The objective is to help Burg find all of the objects scattered across the island. Once you find them all, you win the game! You can find the latest scavenger hunts using the navigation bar at the top of the screen. This site was created using GitHub Pages, Bootstrap 3.4.1, and Glyphicons. It does not collect any user data, but stores a couple variables on the user's browser.

The latest CPZero version this site recognizes is v1.0.5.7




The History of Burg's Hunts


January 22nd, 2026: The first scavenger hunt image was made, and it was decided to make it an official event on Zero Nation. It was headed by samuel, Samster68, and Litdia. You can find the original hunt here.

January 30th, 2026: The first build of this site was made public online, missing a lot of its current features. It was not officially released or announced at this point.

February 12th, 2026: The first YouTube video featuring Burg was released, setting up his story and introducing him to Zero Nation for the first time. The first comic was adjusted slightly to work with this storyline.

Burg's First Scavenger Hunt


Rockhopper has entrusted Burg to load his cargo back onto the Migrator, but he accidently left without him! Get caught up on Burg's misadventures by watching this event's trailer on YouTube, and by reading one of the comics. Burg is looking for helpful penguins to help him find all of the items on his list scattered around Club Penguin Island. Good luck!
