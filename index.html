    About


    What is Burg's Camp?

    Burg's Camp (originally called "Burg's Scavenger Hunts") was conceptualized in January of 2026 on the Zero Nation Discord server. It is designed to be a secondary location for hosting limited time events called "Adventures", fan art, and other fun stuff! This site was created using GitHub Pages, Bootstrap 3.4.1, and Glyphicons. It does not collect any user data.

    Burg's First Scavenger Hunt


    Rockhopper has entrusted Burg to load his cargo back onto the Migrator, but he accidently left without him! Get caught up on Burg's misadventures by watching this event's trailer on YouTube, and by reading one of the comics. Burg is looking for helpful penguins to help him find all of the items on his list scattered around Club Penguin Island. Good luck!

    Most items will be found by simply waddling around the map. However, some may be found in catalogs, books, or magazines. None of the items are found inside of a minigame.




    Beeker's Pi Day Puzzle


    Happy Pi Day! While most penguins are enjoying the sports and competiting to win the Penguin Games, one penguin is spending their time on more academic matters. Beeker is a student that is spending some time on Club Penguin Island to study and learn more about the world. Today, he is trying to estimate pi.

    Pi is a Greek letter that represents a mathematical constant. It is the ratio between the diameter and the circumference of a circle. Even though circles can be different sizes, all circles have the same ratio!

    Today, Beeker wants to figure out what the value of pi is. He has derived a formula, but needs your help to find the variables within it. Simply fill out all of the text fields with numbers that correspond to the clues. Once you have them all correct, you will have found Beeker's pi estimation! (This adventure requires no mathematical skill. The program will make all of the calculations for you.)
